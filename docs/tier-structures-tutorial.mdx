* In this tutorial you will an example of how to set up and execute a Tier Structure Profile, in this specific tutorial you will create a country with states.

In this Tutorial you will be creating a Tier Structure Profile for the United States containing key/value(or label/value in our case) pairs for its States. **Use the example below for a reference.**

## **Tier 1** - country

**Label**: United States of America - **Value**: USA

## **Tier 2** - states

**Label**: Montana - **Value**: MT **Label**: Washington - **Value**: WA **Label**: New York - **Value**: NY

## What you will need for this tutorial

* A modern Web Browser - We recommend [Firefox](https://www.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/new/) or [Chrome](https://www.google.com/chrome/browser/desktop/index.html)
* An online Agilit-e account - [User Registration and Sign In](/docs/user-registration-and-sign-in)
