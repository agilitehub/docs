1. Create a new Keywords profile using the `Create New` button in the "Keywords Profiles" card. This will open a new Keyword profile form.

![1018](/images/docs/4909351-Create-New-Keywords.png "Create-New-Keywords.png")

2. Populate the Keyword profile **Provide the following in the Form General Tab:**

* Profile Key: leave\_type
* Group Name (Optional): Could be "Internal Keywords" or "HR Keywords", etc.

![992](/images/docs/8c8f31e-Profile-Key-Kewords.png "Profile-Key-Kewords.png")

**Provide Label/Value pairs for each of the leaves types:**

* Label: Sick Leave - Value: sick
* Label: Annual Leave - Value: annual
* Label: Family Responsibility Leave - Value: family

![2300](/images/docs/2f18df1-Label-Value-Keywords.png "Label-Value-Keywords.png")

3. Click on "Submit" to save your new Keywords profile.
