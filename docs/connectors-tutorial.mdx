In this tutorial, we are going use the Connectors module to integrate with an online weather service called [OpenWeatherMap](http://openweathermap.org). OpenWeatherMap is a free online service that allows you to easily retrieve weather forecasts in various forms for almost any location across the globe.

Once you've completed this tutorial you will be able to:

* Configure a Connector Profile that connects with OpenWeatherMap
* Configure Connector Routes that calls the following services:
  * Get the *current weather* for *Chicago, Illinois*
* Execute Agilit-e APIs that trigger your Connectors to return the relevant weather forecasts

## What you will need for this tutorial

* A modern Web Browser - We recommend [Firefox](https://www.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/new/) or [Chrome](https://www.google.com/chrome/browser/desktop/index.html)
* An online Agilit-e account - [User Registration and Sign In](/docs/user-registration-and-sign-in)
* An OpenWeatherMap account - [OpenWeatherMap](http://openweathermap.org)
